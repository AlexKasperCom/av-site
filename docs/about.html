About Me

I was born in Ireland and immigrated to Canada as a teenager. I learned my trade in Montréal as an apprentice under the best stylists, influenced by a fashion savvy diverse crew of Americans, French Quebecers and Canadians.

I was running my own salon, downtown Montréal when I met my (now) husband and love brought the life adventure back to Europe again. I chose Berlin to set up shop anew and to learn another language, as always, with a dreadful accent.

After eight years abroad, we returned to Montréal in 2022.
